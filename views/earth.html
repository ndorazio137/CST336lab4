Earth



Earth our home planet remains special in our hearts.

We are the third planet from the Sun and the fifth largest in the system.

Since Earth is the only planet we know of that has liquid water on its surface, it is the only planet we know of that can sustain life.

Earth is the only planet not named after a Greek or Roman god.

Earth simply means "the ground"
