Venus



Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is called Earths twin due to their similarities in size and gravity.

It is the hottest planet in our system, with its dense atmosphere creating a strong greenhouse effect.

Venus is one of two planets that rotate in the opposite direction of the majority of planets in our system.

This planet was named after Aphrodite the Greek Goddess of love and beauty.

It takes the Sun's light only 6 minutes to reach Venus, which rotates around the Sun every 243 Earth days.
